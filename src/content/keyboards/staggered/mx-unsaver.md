Custom MX keyboard and numpad project inspired by vintage Cherry keyboards and the IBM Unsaver. Designed with KiCad for the PCB and Onshape for the case, featuring an integrated USB 2.0 hub with 400mA downstream power delivery and a brass through-weight component for that classic feel.
